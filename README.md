### Project Insights: 

This Power BI project provides insights into passenger behavior, growth trends, and performance against targets across various cities.

#### **Key Highlights:**
- **Passenger Growth & Retention:**
  - Total Passengers: 238K | New Passengers: 177K (74%) | Repeat Passengers: 61K (26%)
  - Steady growth in passengers from January to May, peaking at 12.1K in May.
  - Most repeat passengers take 2-3 trips, indicating potential for loyalty programs.

- **Performance vs. Targets:**
  - High-performing cities: Jaipur (8.58), Mysore (8.70), and Kochi (8.52) exceeded target ratings.
  - Underperforming cities: Surat (6.42) and Lucknow (6.49) fell below targets, highlighting areas for improvement.

- **Customer Ratings:**
  - Mysore achieved the highest rating (8.7), while Surat and Lucknow showed room for service enhancement.
  - Average passenger ratings indicate a need for targeted service quality improvements in lower-rated cities.

#### **Recommendations:**
1. **Improve Retention**: Enhance repeat passenger experience to increase the 26% retention rate.
2. **Focus on Low-Rated Cities**: Implement service improvements in Surat and Lucknow to meet target ratings.
3. **Leverage Growth Trends**: Capitalize on the growing passenger base with seasonal promotions and loyalty incentives.

This analysis offers actionable insights to drive customer satisfaction, increase retention, and enhance service quality.
